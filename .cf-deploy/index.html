The Man Behind the Camp

Twenty-two years.
Three continents.

Third-degree black belt. Founder of a multi-location academy. Coached more than ten thousand students from white belt to world podiums.

For him, it is a genuine honor to design an international BJJ experience so many people have already fallen in love with — and to set the standard for who belongs in the room, how the training runs, and what happens when you step off the mat. He didn't build this to be another camp. He built it to give the standard he holds himself to twenty-nine other people he'd want at his table.
