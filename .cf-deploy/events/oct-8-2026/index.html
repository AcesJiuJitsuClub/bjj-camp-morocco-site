Why this camp

Morocco, one camp at a time.

Each BJJ Camp Morocco moves to a different region of the country. This camp takes us to Rabat — the imperial capital on the Atlantic. Future camps: Marrakech, Essaouira, the Sahara, Tangier. Past athletes rebook to see another side of Morocco.

TRAINING · CONSISTENT EVERY CAMP
The training stays consistent.

Twice-daily sessions led by elite black belts. Real technical depth, hard live rounds, and partners who'll push you. Same caliber, new setting every camp.

RABAT · THIS CAMP
The setting changes.

Rabat is Morocco's quiet capital — Atlantic coast, walkable medina, and a pace that lets you train hard and recover right. Future camps explore the High Atlas, the Sahara, and the coast.

ATHLETES · LONG TABLE
The room is the constant.

Twenty-nine other practitioners, vetted. Black belts to white belts. Long-table dinners that go past midnight, in whichever city we're in.
